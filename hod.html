Message from the HOD

Shaping Engineers, Building Futures

Welcome to the Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering at SIET. Our department stands as a testament to decades of dedication — blending rigorous academics with hands-on experience to shape engineers who are ready for the challenges of tomorrow.

We believe engineering education must go beyond textbooks. That is why our labs are equipped with industry-standard tools, our curriculum is industry-aligned, and our faculty remain active in research and professional development.

Our students go on to work at leading semiconductor, communication, and technology companies — and several have founded startups of their own. This outcome is not accidental; it is the product of a deliberate, student-first approach to education that our entire faculty team is committed to.

I invite students, parents, and industry partners to engage with our department. Together, we can continue building an institution that produces not just engineers, but problem solvers and innovators.
